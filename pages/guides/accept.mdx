## Accept a Wager

Once either you have created a wager, your opponent can decide to accept or reject the conditions of the wager.

If your opponent denies your wager conditions, you can cancel your wager, and withdraw your tokens. 

To accept a wager, nagivate to the "matches" page. If your opponent has created a wager, you will see it as a pending wager. To accept, click the "accept wager" button.
